## Slide 2: Problem Statement

### The Challenge

**Modern AI models** are powerful but often:
- Require massive datasets and compute
- Act as "black boxes" with unclear decision-making
- Overlook the evolution of ML techniques

**Question:** Can we learn from the past to understand the present?

**RetroBrain** compares models across three eras to show how ML evolved and where each approach excels.

## Slide 3: What RetroBrain Does

### A 3-Era ML Comparison Engine

**1980s Models:**
- Logistic Regression
- Naive Bayes (Gaussian & Multinomial)
- Decision Tree (shallow)
- K-means Clustering

**2000s Models:**
- Random Forest
- SVM (Linear + RBF kernels)
- PCA + SVM pipeline

**2020s Models:**
- Gemini API (Large Language Models)

**All models train on the same dataset** for fair comparison!
